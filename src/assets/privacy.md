## 13- Fraud Prevention — Welcome Bonus System

MasterAi grants new users a one-time welcome bonus of game tokens upon first registration. To prevent abuse of this system (where a single individual could repeatedly create and delete accounts using the same email to obtain multiple welcome bonuses), MasterAi implements a privacy-preserving fraud prevention mechanism.

### 13.1 How it works

When a user deletes their account, MasterAi retains a single piece of pseudonymized data: a **cryptographic hash** of the email address used at the time of deletion. The hash is computed using a one-way cryptographic function combined with a server-side secret. This means:

- **The hash is irreversible**: it is mathematically infeasible to reconstruct the original email from the hash.
- **The hash is unique per email**: the same email always produces the same hash, but two different emails produce two completely different hashes.
- **Without the original email, the hash cannot be linked to any individual**: MasterAi cannot use this hash to identify, contact, or profile any user.

When a new user registers, MasterAi computes the hash of their email and checks whether it matches any hash retained from previously deleted accounts (within the 12-month retention window). If a match is found, the welcome bonus is not granted, but the user can still create the account and use all features of MasterAi normally.

### 13.2 Why this is privacy-friendly

- **Minimization**: only one piece of pseudonymized data is retained — no IP, no name, no device ID, no behavioral data.
- **Limited purpose**: the hash is used solely to determine welcome bonus eligibility, never for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.
- **Limited duration**: 12 months maximum, after which automatic deletion occurs.
- **No automated decisions with significant effects**: the user can always create and use their account normally; only the welcome bonus is affected.
- **Pseudonymization (Article 4(5) GDPR)**: the data is processed in a manner that the data subject can no longer be identified without additional information (the original email).

### 13.3 Legal basis

The processing is based on the **legitimate interest** of the Data Controller pursuant to Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR, namely the protection of the welcome bonus system from systematic abuse. The legitimate interest has been balanced against the fundamental rights and freedoms of data subjects, taking into account:

- the limited and pseudonymized nature of the data retained;
- the limited retention period (12 months);
- the absence of any significant effect on the user (the account can still be created and used);
- the user's reasonable expectation that an anti-abuse measure is in place when a welcome bonus is offered.

### 13.4 Your rights regarding fraud prevention data

You can request the early deletion of your fraud prevention hash (before the standard 12-month period) by contacting **privacy@rpgmaster.ai**. The Data Controller will perform a case-by-case balancing of interests pursuant to Article 17(3)(b) GDPR. If the balancing favors deletion, the hash will be removed; otherwise, the Data Controller will explain the reasoning and the hash will remain until natural expiration.

You also have the right to object to this processing under Article 21 GDPR. Such objections will be evaluated in accordance with the same balancing-of-interests test.
